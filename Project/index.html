Biography

The mission of Diamond Business Solution Inc is to serve the community as a non-profit organization providing small business web startup and employment assistance at no cost to the client. At DBS INC, we are passionate about utilizing the resources available to the communities to spark entrepreneurship and economic growth; therefore, fostering community revitalization. By partnering with various levels of government, businesses and higher learning institutions, we aim to empower individuals to blaze a trail for their future.
